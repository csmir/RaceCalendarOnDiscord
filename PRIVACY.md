# Privacy Statement

Race Calendar respects your privacy. We only collect the information that is necessary to provide the Service.

## What we collect

1. **Race Calendar does not collect personal information by default.**
   Race Calendar does not track your activity, store personal profiles, or collect analytics about how you use the Service.

2. **Some technical information may be processed automatically.**
   When interacting with Discord, Race Calendar may receive information that is required for the Service to function, such as server, channel, or message identifiers. This information is only used to process your requests.

3. **Future personalization.**
   Race Calendar may, in the future, use Discord User IDs to offer optional personalized features. If this happens, this Privacy Statement will be updated to explain what is stored, why it is stored, and how it is used.

## What we do not do

1. **Race Calendar does not sell or share your data.**
   Any information processed by Race Calendar is never sold or shared with third parties for advertising or marketing purposes.

2. **Race Calendar does not build user profiles.**
   The goal of the Service is to provide racing calendars, not to monitor its Consumers.

> Race Calendar and its developers are committed to keeping data collection to the absolute minimum required to provide the Service.
